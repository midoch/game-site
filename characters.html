The Boy is a young male and protagonist of Inside. Not much is known about the boy's history, name or age. The Boy's background is mostly shrouded in mystery. All that is known about him is that security guards throughout the game either kill or capture him when he is spotted.

Unlike the controlled, zombie-like people present in the game, the Boy exerts self-consciousness and the ability to critically think. Like any other human, the Boy can walk, run, crawl, swim, and jump. The Boy also displays a surprising amount of strength, able to climb ladders and ropes quickly, pull heavy objects, and lift his body up edges.
